# Tic-Tac-Toe Game

A beautiful, web-based Tic-Tac-Toe game created for the Brno Scrum Workshop. Play against a friend or challenge our smart AI opponent!

## Features

✅ **Complete Implementation** - Fully playable web-based Tic-Tac-Toe game
✅ **Brno Scrum Workshop Banner** - Custom branded header
✅ **Colorful Design** - Beautiful gradient background and modern UI
✅ **New Game Button** - Reset functionality while preserving scores
✅ **AI Player** - Smart AI opponent with strategic gameplay
✅ **Game Modes** - Play vs Human or vs AI
✅ **Score Tracking** - Keeps track of wins and draws
✅ **Responsive Design** - Works on desktop and mobile devices
✅ **Visual Effects** - Hover animations, winning cell highlights, and smooth transitions

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Choose your game mode:
   - **Play vs Human**: Take turns with another player
   - **Play vs AI**: Challenge the computer opponent
3. Click on empty cells to make your move (X goes first)
4. Try to get three of your symbols in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally)
5. Use the "New Game" button to start fresh while keeping your score

## AI Strategy

The AI opponent uses intelligent gameplay:
- **Offensive**: Tries to win when possible
- **Defensive**: Blocks opponent winning moves
- **Strategic**: Prefers center, then corners, then edges
- **Adaptive**: Makes smart decisions based on board state
